Welcome to Gunn's Portable Appliance Testing.

Portable Appliance Testing is where electrical appliances are routinely checked to see whether they are safe for continued use within the workplace by staff or clients / customers.

PAT testing is vital Health & Safety tool for shop's, office's, landlord's or any business premises.

  • 25% of all accidents within the workplace involve electrical appliances = COMMON.

  • PAT Testing helps prevent the use of dangerous appliances = SENSE.

This Is Our COMMON SENSE Approach To Electrical Safety.
