We are a Portable Appliance Testing business based in Minster-on-Sea, Sheerness and currently serve the South East and London Areas.

Our test engineers are qualified to the highest standards, hold City & Guilds 2377 Level 3 qualification for in-service inspection and testing of electrical equipment, we test to the current IEE Code of Practise 3rd Edition by the IET.

We use modern calibrated portable appliance testing equipment from Metrel to test your equipment to ensure the highest standards so you have peace of mind.
